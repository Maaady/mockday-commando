About us

We are a company that develops a content management system, which allows the creation, editing and publication of content on digital platforms and remote pages. Our service facilitates the structuring of an electronic site without the requirement of specialized technical knowledge.

Target audience

We look for medium-sized companies that are in need or have difficulty finding their customers' segmentation, which is defined by marketing, geographic, demographic, psychological and behavioral criteria to offer our service.

Main goal

Provide from the most basic to a few more advanced resources for managing all your digital assets that are used in different marketing channels, such as websites, marketing content, email campaigns, remote stores, blogs and funded research.

Advantages

Saving time and money in creating and maintaining an electronic site; full control over your content and ease of updating at any time; customization of your content for different audiences and segments.

Possibility of creating which sites

Blogs, web courses, forums, social networks, web stores and content portals.

Simple and functional

Mockday Commando was designed to be simple and functional. That is, nothing beyond what your company's managers and teams need. No bureaucracy, distractions or stress triggers of these genres.

Free before subscription

Managers and teams responsible for writing and publishing your company's content will be able to test Mockday Commando for seven days before any subscriptions.

Exclusive features

When compared to other content management systems on the market, Mockday Commando integrates with Google Analytics, through a third-party extension, and leads to our native A/B Testing solution.
